Gestational diabetes is currently the most common medical complications of pregnancy affecting 10-25 % of pregnancies throughout the world.- International Diabetes Association.... If not controlled, it leads to adverse health outcomes including preeclampsia, macrosomia, caessarean delivery and type 2 diabetes in both mother and child.- Centers for Disease Control and Prevention

SweetExpectations

predicting gestational diabetes for timely interventions




About


This AI powered application is designed with an aim of making the 9 months long journey of an expectant woman smooth. It is built to empower physicians (OB/GYN) to predict the risk of gestational diabetes in their patients during early weeks of gestation, typically before week 20. This would promote ample time for interventions reducing risk of adverse health outcomes in both mother and child. This app is tested on women carrying single child with gestational age of less than 20 weeks. To use the app, click on the risk monitor icon in the navigation bar and fill up the questionnaire with patient's details. For accurate prediciton please enter pregestational BMI.The app outputs the risk of a user based on the values. In case of high risk category, early intervention strategies may be recommended.
